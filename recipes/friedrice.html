Fried Rice


Description

Fry not just your rice but also your taste buds with this amazing fried rice dish!

Ingredients

  • 300g Basmati Rice
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Carrots
  • 200g Peas
  • 1 bundle of spring onions
  • 1-2 Spoons of Oil
  • optional: 1-2 Spoons of Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and cook it while preparing the Vegetables. Slice the Onions and carrots into cubes.
  2. Put everything together in a pan with the oil.
  3. Grill it really well with high heat for like 5~10 Minutes
  4. optional: Add some soy sauce at the end and let it stir for 1 minute with lower heat.
  5. Enjoy!
