Lentil Soup


Description

This Lentil Soup is both rich in flavour and has a comforting texture.

The Lentil in combination with the Sweet Potatoe will melt in your mouth like your very first kiss.

Ingredients

  • 250g Red Lentil
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 Sweet Potatoes
  • 1.5l boiled water

Steps

  1. Chop all the Vegetables into cubes.
  2. Put everything together in a hot tub with about 1.5l boiling water
  3. Stir well for 15~20 Minutes
  4. Enjoy!
