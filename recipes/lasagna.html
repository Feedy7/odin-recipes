Completely Normal Lasagna Recipe

How to make a completely normal Lasagna

This is a Recipe for a completely normal Lasagna. It will taste actually how you remember it from your childhood. After the meat, sauce and vegetables have been prepared perfectly normal, we will finish it with 3 layers of cheese and bake it completely normal in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Noodles
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Chop Up the vegetables
  2. Cook Noodles
  3. Grill the Meat
  4. Put it all together in lasagna form
  5. Bake in a oven for 30~35 minutes (or until it looks completely normal)
  6. Enjoy!
